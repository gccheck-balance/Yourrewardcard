Details about using your yourewardcard

Use Online

The yourewardcard is a simple, safe and convenient method of making online payments. You can use your card at any website or app that accepts Visa or Mastercard debit cards.

When shopping online, enter your card number, expiration date, and security code (CVV) just as you would with any other debit or credit card. Your card is protected by the same security standards as other payment cards. Always use secure, trusted websites and avoid sharing your card details by email or phone.

You can also sign in on this site to check your balance and view recent card activity whenever you need to.

ATM Access

Your yourewardcard may allow you to withdraw cash at ATMs and get cash back at the point of sale, depending on your card type and the Cardholder Agreement that applies to your card.

At ATMs that accept Visa or Mastercard debit cards, you can typically withdraw available funds. Fees may apply for ATM withdrawals or for using out-of-network ATMs. Check your Cardholder Agreement for fee details and daily limits.

When making a purchase at a store, you may be able to request cash back at the register if the merchant offers this option and your card allows it.

Use Everywhere*

Your yourewardcard can be used everywhere Debit Mastercard and Visa debit cards are accepted within the United States and the District of Columbia. That includes retail stores, gas stations, online shopping, bill payments, and more.

Some card programs may have restrictions (for example, online-only or certain merchant categories). See your Cardholder Agreement for full details on where your card can be used, any limits, and applicable terms.

*Terms and eligibility depend on your specific card program. Funds and usage are subject to the Cardholder Agreement.
